About Bella Vista

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi Family, bella vista has been serving authentic italian cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingedients has been made us a beloved destination for food lovers.

Every dish is prepared with love and attention to adult using recipes passed down through generation of italian chefs.
